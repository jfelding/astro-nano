## Retinning antique copper cookware:
- Restores functionality and aesthetics
- Enhances heat conductivity
- Bare copper is toxic!
- 'Big market' for restoring high-quality stuff

## Project ideas:
- Restore heirloom kitchenware
- Revitalize vintage collections

## Equipment and materials:
- Foodgrade tin lining, flux
- Copper polish, protective gear
- Heat source, ventilation
