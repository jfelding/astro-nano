## Amber glass and glasses

Glass can be used for:

- Plant roots in water without high-frequency light that allow algae to grow (this is why it has been used for medicine containers)

- Amber glasses can be used to suppress the blue light from screens that affect our sleep. 

- Amber glasses make things look nice and warm!
